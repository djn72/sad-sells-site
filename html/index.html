About Sad Sells

Manchester-based and soul-stirring, Sad Sells is a five-piece modern folk rock gem.

Influenced by Big Thief, Radiohead, and Phoebe Bridgers, their music captures raw emotions within a blend of haunting melodies and dynamic rhythms. With an acoustic heartbeat and electric soul, Sad Sells invites you to experience their introspective journey through a landscape of vulnerability.
